Lead Security Engineer with +5 years of experience in defensive security, built on a 9-year foundation in engineering, automation, and infrastructure across large-scale, highly regulated corporate environments.

Specialized in **Endpoint Security** and **Detection Engineering**, with hands-on experience building controls, integrating security tooling ecosystems (EDR, SIEM, SOAR, CTI), proactive threat hunting, and risk-driven metrics. Experienced in elevating the operational maturity of security tools from MITRE ATT&CK coverage mapping to MISP enrichment pipelines and systematic detection tuning.

Strong technical background in infrastructure, automation, and development, enabling deep engagement in both strategy definition and hands-on execution. Experience with build vs. buy evaluations, technical team leadership, and regulated environments (PCI DSS, SOX, LGPD).

Currently studying and building practical applications of LLMs in defensive security, with a growing interest in **AI Security** both applying AI to improve defenses and ensuring security in AI systems themselves. Hands-on projects with **Claude Code**, **GitHub Copilot**, and **Cursor**.

Special student in the M.Sc. Computer Science program at UEL, working towards full enrollment to develop research on vulnerabilities introduced by AI-powered coding assistants.
